# Results and certificate data

`constructed_witness_certificate.json` and `constructed_witness_certificate/` are the canonical
proof-bearing certificate records for the explicit witness. `quantitative_degeneracy_bound.json`
records the certified numerical benchmark stated in the manuscript.

Other check summaries are generated by `run_all.sh` and are intentionally ignored by Git. They are
convenience outputs, not independent evidence and not part of the citation record.
